About Joshua fashions.

Joshau Fashions is a cloth making company, that has both a factory and an outlet shop. It was started on 6th-feb-2023 by a man called called Joshua Tihabwangye and his former Tr of fashion and design, Mureeba Frank. It was started from a place called Nyakagyezi in kanungu district but later on it was shifted and taken to Kisaasi in kampala. The business was funded by the family of Mr and Mrs Pr.Byarugaba Fred in partnership with Baclays bank. The business wa named after the owner's name Joshau which is one of the names of the owner "Joshua Tihabwangye"


Leadership

  • Owners: Joshau Tihabwangye and Mureeba Frank.
  • C.E.O: Ankunda Freedom.
  • Permanent Secretary: Tumusiime Elijah.
  • Public Relations Officer: Lupya Jessy.

Mission

To provide quality standard clothing to all Ugandans and non Ugandans in this generation.

Objectives

  • To be the leading producer and distributor of trending fashions and design in clothings.
  • To create employment opportunities to most of unemployed youths of Uganda
  • To provide market to uganda's cotton that has a small market on both the local sssssand international market
